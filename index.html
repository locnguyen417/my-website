About USAFV

The USAFV Armed Forces is a dedicated military organization committed to national security, service, and honor. We uphold the values of courage, integrity, and unity across all our operations.

Branches

  • Army Division
  • Air Command
  • Naval Unit
  • Special Operations Forces
  • Cybersecurity Command

Missions

Our missions include national defense, international peacekeeping, humanitarian aid, disaster response, and technological innovation to protect and advance the interests of our nation.

Recruitment

We are looking for dedicated individuals who are ready to serve. Visit our recruitment center to explore careers, eligibility criteria, and benefits.

Veteran Services

USAFV honors the service of all veterans. We offer support programs, healthcare access, job assistance, and retirement benefits to those who have served.
